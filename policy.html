    There could be certain circumstances beyond our control where you could receive a damaged / defective product or a product that is not the same as per your original order.     Conditions for Return

  • Please notify us of receipt of a damaged / defective product within maximum 24 hours of delivery.
  • Products/Items should be unused.
  • Products should be returned in their original packaging along with the original price tags, labels and invoices.
  • The product should be couriered to our office address as mentioned by the customer support team, depending on the originating warehouse from where the product was dispatched. Courier charges to be borne by the customer.
  • It is advised that the return packets should be strongly and adequately packaged so that there is no further damage of goods in transit.
  • Once the goods are received at the Company Name center, we will examine the product and if the claim is found genuine, we will refund the product price to your bank account, while the delivery charges (twice the initial delivery fee charged) would be given to you as a voucher that can be used on the website in the future. If the claim is found not valid, you will have to pay the delivery fees again for getting the product delivered back.
  • We do not have an exchange policy for sizes.
  • We do not have an exchange policy for used products, no matter how rarely used.

    • Refunds

  • We will process the refund after receipt of the product by WW/B or its business partner. Refund will be processed based on the mode of payment of the order
  • Orders paid by credit / debit card will be refunded to the credit / debit card or your bank account within 7 working days and the refund will reflect in the next statement.
  • Orders paid by net banking accounts will be credited back to bank account.
  • For all other modes of payment, we will send a refund cheque. The cheque will be made in favor of the name as in the "billing name" provided at the time of placing the order.
